Kibicara

Kibicara hoods are connecting your local community - while you use your favorite messenger.

Find your hood!

Cross-platform

Kibicara provides a connection to your community, without the need to install new software. This minimizes the barrier of entry for new users.

Anonymous

Messages sent with Kibicara are anonymous - perfect for news tickers, activism or disaster warnings

Community-driven

Hoods aren't centrally administrated - Admins can customize the behavior of their hoods to their hearts content.

Kibicara is a bridge between multiple messaging platforms with the goal of providing anoymous communication for (local) communities. Hoods are community-administrated instances of kibicara, being able to blacklist or whitelist certain phrases. You subscribe to a hood through your service of choice. This can either be a E-Mail, Telegram or Twitter.
