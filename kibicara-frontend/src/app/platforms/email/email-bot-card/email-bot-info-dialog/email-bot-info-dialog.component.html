How to communicate with the hood via E-Mail?

How to subscribe to the hood via E-Mail?
  1. Enter your E-Mail address in the input field below Subscribe to E-Mail list. This will send you a confirmation mail.
  2. Check your E-mail inbox for an e-mail with the confirmation link. Click the link to confirm your subscription. Done!
How to send a broadcast message to the hood?

Write an e-mail to one of the addresses below the Send e-mail to hood label. Your message should be in the body of the e-mail, the rest (e.g. Subject) is irrelevant and will be stripped.

How to receive messages?

Subscribe to the hood and you will automatically receive e-mails when someone sends a broadcast message on another platform.

How to stop receiving messages?

In each e-mail you receive from the hood there is also a link to unsubscribe to the hood. Just click it to unsubscribe.
